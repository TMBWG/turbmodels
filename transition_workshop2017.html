NASA Transition Modeling Workshop 2017

NASA organized a Transition Modeling Workshop on September 13-14, 2017, which was held in the Thimble Shoal Auditorium at the Lockheed Martin Center for Innovation ("The Lighthouse") at 8000 Harbour View Boulevard in Suffolk, VA 23435. The purpose of the workshop was to help assess the challenges associated with the modeling of boundary-layer transition as related to the objectives of CFD Vision 2030 and to formulate a long term research plan to overcome those challenges. The workshop included invited presentations by experts and stakeholders from government, industry, and academia, and concluded with an open forum discussion that was facilitated by Prof. Eli Reshotko and Dr. Phillipe Spalart.

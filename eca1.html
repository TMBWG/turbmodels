Information from Lisbon "Workshop on CFD Uncertainty Analysis" series

This web page provides some information from a series of turbulence-related Validation and Verification workshops held in Lisbon, Portugal, at the Instituto Superior Tecnico (IST). It includes manufactured solutions for wall-bounded incompressible turbulent flow. Everything on this page was provided courtesy of the workshop organizer Luis Eca, of IST. NASA assumes no responsibility for the accuracy of this information; questions should be directed to the originator. Additional details about the three workshops can be found in the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics papers AIAA-2005-4728 (Toronto, June 2005), https://doi.org/10.2514/6.2005-4728 AIAA-2007-4089 (Miami, June 2007), https://doi.org/10.2514/6.2007-4089 and AIAA-2009-3647 (San Antonio, June 2009), https://doi.org/10.2514/6.2009-3647. See also Int. J. Numer. Meth. Fluids 54(2):119-154, 2007, https://doi.org/10.1002/fld.1387 and Int. J. Computational Fluid Dynamics 21(3-4):175-188, 2007, https://doi.org/10.1080/10618560701553436 for details on the construction of manufactured solutions for one- and two-equation eddy-viscosity turbulence models.

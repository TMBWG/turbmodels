       7.     INSTRUMENTATION                                                   
              SURFACE PRESSURE MEASUREMENTS:                                    
              PRESSURE HOLE SIZES - 0.00035 M DIAMETER AT X/C=0 TO 0.01875,     
              0.00065 M DIAMETER AT X/C=0.02709 TO 0.95, 0.00035 M              
              DIAMETER AT X/C=0.975 TO 1.0                                      
              TYPE OF TRANSDUCER AND SCANNING DEVICES - DIFFERENTIAL-           
              PRESSURE-CAPSULE-MANOMETERS WITH STAGNATION REFERENCE             
              PRESSURE, 1 ATMOSPHERE RANGE CALIBRATED TO GIVE + OR -            
              0.03% FULL SCALE DISPLACEMENT (FSD) ACCURACY; PRESSURE            
              READINGS FROZEN WHEN STEADY AND THEN SCANNED                      
              WAKE MEASUREMENTS:                                                
              TYPE/SIZE - 0.0005 M OD, 0.00025 M ID PITOT, 0.001 M OD           
              STATIC PROBE                                                      
              TYPE OF TRANSDUCER AND SCANNING DEVICES - DIFFERENTIAL-           
              PRESSURE-CAPSULE-MANOMETERS USED FOR PRESSURE MEASUREMENTS;       
              POSITIONAL MEASUREMENTS TAKEN WITH THIN-FILM POTENTIOMETERS       
              AT X/C=1.0 AND 1.025 CALIBRATED TO + OR - 0.00005 M ACCURACY      
              AND TUNNEL CENTER-LINE-RIG ROLL-UNIT AT X/C=2.0 GIVING            
              + OR - 0.0005 M ACCURACY                                          
              BOUNDARY LAYER MEASUREMENTS:                                      
              TYPE/SIZE - PITOT - RECTANGULAR (X/C <0.6) 0.0012 M X 0.00015 M   
              ORIFICE, CIRCULAR (X/C>0.6) 0.0005 M OD AND 0.00025 M ID;         
              0.001 M OD STATIC PROBE                                           
              TYPE OF TRANSDUCER AND SCANNING DEVICES - DIFFERENTIAL-           
              PRESSURE-CAPSULE-MANOMETERS USED FOR PRESSURE MEASUREMENTS;       
              POSITIONAL MEASUREMENTS TAKEN WITH THIN-FILM POTENTIOMETERS       
              CALIBRATED TO GIVE AN ACCURACY OF TRAVEL OF 0.1% FSD FOR          
              A RANGE VARYING BETWEEN 0.01 AND 0.04 M

       9.     MEASURED VARIABLES:                                               
              X - CHORDWISE ORDINATE, DATUM AT LEADING EDGE (M)                 
              XUS - X UPPER SURFACE (M)                                         
              XLS - X LOWER SURFACE (M)                                         
              Y - SECTION ORDINATE TAKEN NORMAL TO AIRFOIL SURFACE (M)          
              YUS - Y UPPER SURFACE (M)                                         
              YLS - Y LOWER SURFACE (M)                                         
              ALPHAG - GEOMETRIC ANGLE OF INCIDENCE (DEGREES)                   
              ALPHAC - ALPHA CORRECTED (DEGREES)                                
              CN - NORMAL FORCE COEFFICIENT                                     
              CM - PITCHING MOMENT COEFFICIENT                                  
              CD - DRAG COEFFICIENT                                             
              PSTAT - AIRFOIL SURFACE STATIC PRESSURE (N/M**2)                  
              QREF - TOTAL HEAD PRESSURE (N/M**2)                               
              CP - AIRFOIL SURFACE PRESSURE COEFFICIENT                         
              Z - SPANWISE ORDINATE, DATUM .076 M OUTSIDE WORKING SECTION       
              AT TUNNEL ROOF (M)                                                
              CPBL - BOUNDARY LAYER STATIC PRESSURE COEFFICIENT                 
              U - MEAN STREAMWISE VELOCITY (M/SEC)                              
              UE - VELOCITY EXTERNAL TO BOUNDARY LAYER OBTAINED FROM            
              THE PITOT PRESSURE MEASURED EXTERNAL TO THE BOUNDARY LAYER        
              OR WAKE AND STATIC PRESSURE MEASURED AT THE APPROPRIATE           
              VALUE OF Y/C (M/SEC)                                              
              CF - FRICTION COEFFICIENT                                         
              DELS - DISPLACEMENT THICKNESS, CORRECTED FOR NORMAL STATIC        
              PRESSURE GRADIENT WHEN MEASURED (M)                               
              THETA - MOMENTUM THICKNESS, CORRECTED FOR NORMAL STATIC           
              PRESSURE GRADIENT WHEN MEASURED (M)                               
              H - SHAPE FACTOR (DELS/THETA)

       11.    TAPE ORGANIZATION:                                                
                   THE TAPE IS A 2400 FOOT, 9 TRACK, ODD PARITY, PHASE          
              ENCODED, UNLABELLED TAPE WRITTEN AT A DENSITY OF 1600 BITS        
              PER INCH ACCORDING TO EBCDIC CODE.  THE RECORD FORMAT IS          
              FIXED AND BLOCKED; RECORD LENGTH=80 BYTES; 100 RECORDS            
              PER BLOCK; BLOCKSIZE=8000 BYTES.                                  
                                                                                
                   NORMALIZED DATA ARE CREATED FROM MEASURED DATA AS FOLLOWS:   
              XNORM=(X-XMIN)/(XMAX-XMIN)                                        
              NORMALIZED VALUES ARE INTEGERIZED BY MULTIPLYING BY 10000         
              AND ROUNDING UP OR DOWN TO THE NEAREST INTEGER                    
              IXNORM=XNORM*10000                                                
              THUS EACH NORMALIZED AND INTEGERIZED DATUM IS WRITTEN ONTO        
              TAPE AS A NUMBER BETWEEN 0 AND 10000.  ALL NULL DATA ARE          
              WRITTEN AS 20000.  THE EQUATION DESCRIBING THE RELATION           
              BETWEEN ACTUAL DATA AND THE NORMALIZED DATA ON TAPE IS            
              X=XMIN+(((XMAX-XMIN)*IXNORM)/10000)                               
              WHERE X, XMAX AND XMIN ARE REAL AND IXNORM IS AN INTEGER.

4.  DATE AND LOCATION OF EXPERIMENT:  1966, I.M.S.T., MARSEILLE.

5.  ABSTRACT:  THIS EXPERIMENTAL PROGRAM WAS CARRIED OUT IN A SPECIAL
    TEST SECTION AT I.M.S.T.  THE VELOCITY OUTSIDE THE BOUNDARY LAYER
    WAS ESSENTIALLY CONSTANT AT 11 M/S AND THE BOUNDARY LAYER WAS
    ALLOWED TO GROW ON A SOLID SURFACE 1.026 M. LONG FOLLOWED BY FLOW
    OVER A POROUS SURFACE 0.92 M. LONG.  TESTS WERE MADE AT A SERIES
    OF SUCTION RATES UP TO  F = -0.0144.  FOR SOME OF THE LAYERS
    VELOCITY PROFILES WERE MEASURED ALONG THE POROUS SURFACE AT VARIOUS
    STATIONS BUT ONLY VALUES OF THE INTEGRATED PARAMETERS (I. E.
    DISPACEMENT THICKNESS, MOMENTUM THICKNESS AND H) ARE AVAILABLE.
    FOR OTHER SUCTION RATES (F = 0, -0.0020, -0.0053, -0.0099 AND
    -0.0144) TURBULENCE MEASUREMENTS (U1U1, V1V1 AND U1V1) WERE
    MEASURED AT ONE STATION 0.4 M DOWNSTREAM OF THE START OF SUCTION.
    IT IS SUGGESTED THAT THIS TURBULENCE DATA FORM THE BASIS OF A
    SIMPLE DATA SET STARTING FROM A GIVEN VELOCITY PROFILE ON THE
    SOLID SURFACE 50 MM UPSTREAM OF THE START OF SUCTION.  THE
    OUTPUT OF THE CALCULATIONS COULD THEN BE COMPARED WITH THE
    MEASURED TURBULENCE PROFILES AT X = 400 MM.  IN ADDITION
    VELOCITY PROFILES AT X = 400 MM ARE AVAILABLE FOR THREE SUCTION
    RATES (F = 0, -0.0052, AND -0.0142) AND THESE COULD BE USED AS A
    FURTHER CHECK ON THE CALCULATIONS.

    RHO = 0.1228 KG/M**3
    MU  = 1.842*10**-6 KG/M-SEC

8.  EXPERIMENTAL PARAMETERS:  THE TESTS WERE MADE AT A UNIFORM VELOCI-
    TY OF 11 M/S ON A TEST SURFACE 2.0 M LONG.  THE POROUS SURFACE
    STARTED 1.026 M DOWNSTREAM OF THE BEGINNING OF THE TEST SURFACE
    AND THE POROUS SURFACE WAS 0.235 M WIDE AND DID NOT SPAN THE TEST
    SECTION.  THE HEIGHT OF THE TEST SECTION ABOVE THE TEST SURFACE
    WAS ABOUT 0.5 M.  THE VARIATIONS IN THE POROSITY OF THE TEST SURFACE
    ARE QUOTED AS +/- 2.5% ALONG THE PLATE AND +/- 12% ACROSS THE PLATE.
    THE INITIAL CONDITIONS FOR ALL LAYERS AT X = - 0.05 M WERE THETA =
    0.0022 M AND H = 1.33.  THE BEGINNING OF THE POROUS SURFACE WAS
    CONSIDERED THE ORIGIN (X = 0.0).

Shown below are time-resolved PIV data from Test 640 (mp4 format) of u-velocity in the plane y=-241.1mm, cutting through the corner separation. The purpose here is to show the unsteadiness of the separated flow. The PIV are 2-D, and the movies are made up of instantaneous frames with image acquisition rate of 742 Hz. (From T640, 5 degree angle of incidence.) Some details can be found in: AIAA-2019-0078.

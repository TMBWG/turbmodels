The hole for the jet is located at the bottom of a wind tunnel test section 15.0 inches (381.00 mm) wide by approximately 9.81 inch (249.17 mm) high (distance from the splitter plate to the top wall). The height of the top wall is adjusted down the length of the test section to yield a flow with an approximately zero streamwise pressure gradient. The following links point to two CAD files for the Case 2 geometry, one in IGES format, and one in UG (Unigraphics V.18) format (note that the UG file is not a formatted file):

  UG FILE (82 Kb)
 
 

Note that the geometry information is based on theoretical (planned, blueprint-type) specifications. If available, QA data will be given to show differences between the theoretical and as-built configurations.
